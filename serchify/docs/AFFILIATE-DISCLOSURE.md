## What Are Affiliate Links?

An affiliate link is a special URL that includes a tracking code. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, the seller knows you came from Serchify and may pay us a small commission.

**Example:**

- **Original URL**: `https://www.aliexpress.com/item/123456.html`
- **Affiliate URL**: `https://www.aliexpress.com/item/123456.html?aff_trace_key=serchify&...`

The extra parameters (`?aff_trace_key=...`) are the affiliate tracking codes.

## How Affiliate Links Work in Serchify

### 1. It's Optional

Affiliate links are **OFF by default**. You must explicitly enable them in Settings.

**To Enable:**

2. Check "Enable affiliate links"
3. Click Save Settings

**To Disable:**
1. Click Settings
2. Uncheck "Enable affiliate links"

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Will enabling affiliate links make results biased?

**A:** No. Results are ranked by:
1. Relevance/confidence score
2. Price (if you sort by price)
3. Ratings

Whether a platform has an affiliate program doesn't affect ranking.

### Q: Do you hide cheaper options to push affiliate links?

**A:** Absolutely not. We show all results we find, sorted by match quality and price. Our goal is to help you find the best deals, regardless of affiliate status.

### Q: What if I enable affiliate links then change my mind?

**A:** Just disable in Settings. Already-clicked links will still have tracking codes, but future clicks will be clean URLs.

### Q: Can I enable affiliate links for some platforms but not others?

**A:** Currently it's all-or-nothing, but we're considering per-platform controls in the future.

### Q: Do you make more money if I buy expensive items?

**A:** Commission is percentage-based, so technically yes. But our goal is helping you save money - we'd rather you find a cheaper option that saves you $50 than a pricier one that earns us an extra dollar.

### Q: What if I use an ad blocker or privacy tool?

**A:** Some privacy tools strip tracking parameters. That's okay! Your choice to use privacy tools is more important than our affiliate earnings.
