### Q: Can I do this with less budget?
**A**: Possible but risky. You could:
- Start with iOS only (save ~20%)
- Outsource development (save ~30% but higher risk)
- Skip some features (reduce scope)
- Extend timeline (reduce burn rate)

**Not recommended**: Quality critical for children's app.

### Q: What's the biggest risk?
**A**: Three major risks:
1. **Voice recognition** might not work well with 4-6 year-olds
2. **Engagement** - kids might not stay engaged
3. **Market** - parents might not pay

**Mitigation**: User testing BEFORE full development (Phase 0).

**A**: Depends on your goals:
- ‚úÖ **First-mover** in pre-literate coding
- ‚úÖ **Large market** (20M+ children in US)

- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Unproven market** (needs validation)

### Q: Can I build this myself / with small team?
**A**: Challenging. Requires expertise in:
- React Native development
- Backend/API development
- Voice integration (TTS/STT)
- Child UX design
- Early childhood education
- Animation/motion design
- COPPA compliance
