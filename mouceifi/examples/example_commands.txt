# Example Commands for Mouceifi

# Simple movement
move to x=100 y=100
move to x=500, y=300

# Relative movement
move 100 pixels down
move 50 pixels right
move 100 pixels down and 50 pixels right

# Click actions
left click
right click
middle click
double click
right click at current position

# Movement with click
move to x=500 y=300 and then left click
move to x=100 y=100 then right click

# Drag operations
drag from x=100 y=100 to x=300 y=300
drag from x=0 y=0 to x=500 y=500

# Scrolling
scroll down
scroll up
scroll down by 5
scroll up by 10
scroll left
scroll right by 3

# Complex sequences
move to x=200 y=200 then left click then move 50 pixels right then double click
move to x=100 y=100 and drag to x=300 y=300
